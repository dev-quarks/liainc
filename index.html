Understanding Public Procurement

Public procurement involves the process by which government agencies and organizations purchase goods, services, and construction services. It ensures that public funds are spent responsibly, transparently, and fairly, supporting community development and economic growth. Understanding these processes helps businesses navigate opportunities and fosters trust between the public sector and private sector partners.
