About LIA Procurement Inc.

Indigenous-Led
Procurement Excellence

LIA Procurement Inc. is an Indigenous-owned company specializing in public sector procurement at federal, provincial, and municipal levels across Canada. We actively participate in public tenders and government solicitations, serving as both prime contractor and strategic intermediary.

We built LIA to solve the very challenges our communities once faced. Today, we honor trust, uplift voices, and walk forward together — one partnership at a time.

Our flexible, partnership-driven model ensures the right expertise meets each opportunity — grounded in Indigenous values of respect, reciprocity, and relationship.

What We Do

Comprehensive
Procurement Services

End-to-end support across the full procurement lifecycle — from strategy through award through delivery.

01

Procurement Strategies

Expert guidance navigating complex public sector tenders — federal, provincial, and municipal — leveraging our Indigenous procurement advantage to unlock exclusive set-aside opportunities and scoring advantages.

02

Bid Preparation

Comprehensive assistance with proposal development, technical writing, compliance review, and submission management — crafted to maximize evaluation scores and win rates across competitive solicitations.

03

Contract Management

End-to-end post-award support to ensure compliance, quality delivery, stakeholder communication, and successful project completion — protecting your reputation and relationships with government clients.

04

Business Development

Strategic market mapping, opportunity identification, teaming and subcontracting arrangements, and pipeline development to build a sustainable public sector revenue stream.
