About LIA Procurement Inc.

Indigenous-Led Procurement Excellence

LIA Procurement Inc. is an Indigenous-owned company specializing in public sector procurement at federal, provincial, and municipal levels across Canada. We actively participate in public tenders and government solicitations, serving as both prime contractor and strategic intermediary.

We built LIA Procurement Inc. to solve the very challenges we once faced in our own communities. Today, we don't just deliver solutions — we honor trust, uplift voices, and walk forward together, one partnership at a time.

Our flexible, partnership-driven model allows us to match the right expertise to each opportunity, ensuring successful outcomes for government clients and business partners alike. We bring forward Indigenous values of respect, reciprocity, and relationship-building in all our engagements.
