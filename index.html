About Us

LIA Procurement Inc. is a trailblazing firm that combines procurement expertise with strategic insight to transform how businesses engage with Canada’s public sector landscape.

From day one, our mission has been guided by more than just business — it’s been about responsibility, community, and connection to land and people. As an Indigenous-led company, we bring forward the values of respect, reciprocity, and relationship-building in everything we do.

We built this company to solve the very challenges we once faced in our own communities. Today, we don’t just deliver solutions — we honor trust, uplift voices, and walk forward together, one partnership at a time.

Partner With Us

We specialize in identifying, securing, and managing public sector contracts across all levels of government in Canada. We act as both a prime contractor and a strategic intermediary — connecting capable subcontractors with real opportunities.

Our Indigenous ownership positions us to contribute meaningfully to Canada’s evolving procurement strategy, with partnerships grounded in trust and shared success.

Sectors & Capabilities
